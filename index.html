Frequently asked questions

A buyer's agent represents the buyer in a property purchase. Property Wizards works exclusively for buyers, never sellers, and accepts no commission from sellers or developers, so our research, advice and negotiation are entirely in your interest.

Property Wizards holds 11 Buyer's Agent of the Year titles, was named National Buyer's Agent of the Year in 2020 and 2022, won REIWA Buyer's Agent of the Year for six consecutive years (2019–2024), and has been inducted into the REIWA Buyer's Agent Hall of Fame.

We buy across Perth and Western Australia for local, interstate and overseas buyers, including expats, investors, developers and self-managed super funds (SMSFs).

Under our 10-point no-risk guarantee, you only pay a buying fee if we find a property you like. We negotiate the lowest possible price and best terms, and manage the process through to settlement.
